The History

HW Fitness was founded my the Medley Family in 2011. The first gym is located in Charlotte, NC the home gym. The Medley Family continues to add more locations year after year. The foundation of HW Fitness is "Hard Work pays off", thus given the gyn name Hard Work Fitness.

Amenities

Each gym is equipped with the following:

  • Olympic size pool
  • 6 outdoor multi-purpose fields
  • 2 Indoor multipurpose fields
  • Boxing gym
  • 4 Basketball courts
  • 4 private workout studios
  • Full service smoothie bar
